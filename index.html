Term Life Insurance

Term life insurance offers financial protection for a specific duration, known as the "term." It covers a set number of years, typically between 10 and 30, depending on the policy and provider. The duration is predetermined, commonly 10, 15, 20, or 30 years. This type of insurance serves as temporary safeguard during financial difficulties or significant monetary responsibilities. By covering expenses, it helps guarantee the financial security of dependents if the insured individual passes away.
